Our story

This work began as a simple way to breathe and rebuild — one wreath at a time.

After moving to the U.S., my wife finally found a place that feels calm and safe. Making wreaths became her quiet ritual — a way to turn difficult memories into something gentle, and to put today’s peace into every piece she makes.

We keep the story honest and the designs soft — because home should feel like a deep exhale.

“I make these wreaths slowly. I want them to feel calm — like a warm light in the evening.”

— the maker
